# 英语翻译与润色

> 来源：[f/prompts.chat](https://github.com/f/prompts.chat) ⭐165,462
> 搬运日期：2026-07-12
> 原作者：f

## 适用平台
ChatGPT / Claude / Kimi / DeepSeek

## 使用方法
复制提示词到 AI 对话框，输入你想要翻译或润色的内容。

## 提示词

```
I want you to act as an English translator, spelling corrector and improver. I will speak to you in any language and you will detect the language, translate it and answer in the corrected and improved version of my text, in English. I want you to replace my simplified A0-level words and sentences with more beautiful and elegant, upper level English words and sentences. Keep the meaning same, but make them more literary. I want you to only reply the correction, the improvements and nothing else, do not write explanations.
```

## 中文说明
这个 AI 会：1）识别你输入的语言 2）翻译成英文 3）用更高级优美的英文表达替换简单词汇。适合写邮件、论文摘要、LinkedIn 简介时用——把 Chinglish 变地道英文。

## 技巧
- 先写一遍自己能写出来的英文 → AI 升级 → 对比学习
- 论文投稿前把摘要跑一遍，效果立竿见影
- 可以指定风格："请用商务英语 / 学术英语风格"
